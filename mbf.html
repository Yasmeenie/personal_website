MY BEST FRIENDS



In 2022, we decided to hang out after finishing our final SPM (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia) paper. This photo was taken just after the exam.One of my closest pals from high school is this one; the person next to her, named "Zarifah," is wearing purple. Also, "Zainatul" is the one who displays mini-love, and "Namira" is the person who stands next to her.

I never had true best friends when I was in middle school since I constantly changed my best friends every year. I'm not sure why I did this; perhaps it's a process of discovering a best friend. However, these are my genuine best friends that have lasted longer.It's so difficult to locate true best friends; I have to go to the people that betray and bully me.My closest friends have always been supportive of me and nice to me in everything.They enjoy my second home, which is where I go to get compliments, laugh, have fun, unwind, and more.It's surprising that in our eight years of friendship, we have never argued with one another. We all just need a good vibe in our group and we all understand one other so much.


This is actually us after we haven't hanged out in a long time. We are all confined in our houses throughout the COVID era, so this is our first time seeing each other.
